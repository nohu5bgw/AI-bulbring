The Opportunity

Paper receipts are broken. We fixed them.

The Problem

Paper receipts fail everyone

  • Wasteful — BPA-coated thermal paper, non-recyclable
  • Customers lose them immediately
  • Email receipts get ignored and clog inboxes
  • Merchants pay for paper rolls constantly
  • No data — zero insight into customer behaviour
The Solution

QuitSlip — tap and done

  • NFC sticker at checkout — customer taps their phone
  • Receipt opens instantly in the browser, no app needed
  • Saved, shareable, downloadable as an image
  • Zero paper, zero ink, zero recurring supply cost
  • Merchant gets anonymized transaction data
How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

🏷️
Step 1

Merchant places sticker

A pre-programmed NFC sticker is placed at the till or on the card reader. One-time setup, no hardware changes.

📲
Step 2

Customer taps

After paying, the customer taps their phone on the sticker. Any modern iPhone or Android works — no app required.

🧾
Step 3

Receipt appears

A fully itemized digital receipt loads in the browser in under a second. Save it, screenshot it, or share it.

Why It Wins

Value for both sides of the counter

🏪

For Merchants

Eliminate paper roll costs, reduce printer maintenance, and get clean transaction data from every tap. Works alongside your existing POS — no integration needed to get started.

👤

For Customers

No app to download, no account to create. Receipts are stored on the device, easy to screenshot, and available for returns. A genuinely better experience in seconds.

🌱

Sustainability

The average retail location prints 15,000+ receipts a year. QuitSlip eliminates that waste entirely — no thermal paper, no BPA, no landfill.

⚡

Zero Friction Adoption

Unlike QR codes or email receipts, there's nothing to opt into. The sticker works with every modern smartphone out of the box, including offline-capable receipts.
